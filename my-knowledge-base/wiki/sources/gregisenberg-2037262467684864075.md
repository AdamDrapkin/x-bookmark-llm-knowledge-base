## Summary

Greg Isenberg shares an intriguing encounter with the mysterious creator behind [[Paperclip]], an open-source project he describes as one of the fastest-growing in the AI space. The creator notably refuses to reveal his identity publicly ("won't show his face"). Isenberg's thread outlines a practical framework for using Paperclip to deploy multiple [[AI agents]] as a functioning startup team—with [[CEO]], engineer, and other specialized roles handled entirely by autonomous agents. The core premise challenges traditional startup structure: rather than hiring humans for each function, Paperclip enables a single founder (or no one) to orchestrate a company through coordinated AI agents that can execute business operations independently. This approach represents a growing trend toward fully autonomous business operations, where [[agent orchestration]], [[task delegation]], and [[business process automation]] replace conventional hiring.
