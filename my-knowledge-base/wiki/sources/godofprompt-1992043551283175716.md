# Gemini 3.0 Prompting Tips

**Author:** [God of Prompt](https://x.com/godofprompt/status/1992043551283175716)
**Date:** November 22, 2025
**Views:** 17.1K
**Bookmarks:** 173

## Overview

Five key prompting tips for Gemini 3.0 based on how it was designed to be used.

## Tips

### 1. High-Level Descriptions Win
Gemini 3 excels at "vibe coding" where you describe WHAT you want, not HOW to build it.

### 2. Keep Temperature at 1.0
Unlike other models, Gemini 3 needs high entropy (default temp 1.0) for complex reasoning. Lowering it breaks the model's ability to reason creatively.

### 3. Simplify Prompts
Remove complex chain-of-thought engineering you used for Gemini 2.5. Let the model reason naturally with:
> thinking_level: "high"

### 4. Less Verbose by Default
Gemini 3 gives direct, efficient answers. If you want chatty/conversational, explicitly request it.

### 5. Context at the End
For large datasets (codebases, books, videos), put your question/instruction AFTER the data, not before.
