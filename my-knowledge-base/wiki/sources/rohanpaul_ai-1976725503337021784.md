# Mind Your Tone: Investigating How Prompt Politeness Affects LLM Accuracy

**Author:** [Rohan Paul](https://x.com/rohanpaul_ai/status/1976725503337021784)
**Date:** October 10, 2025
**Views:** 885.6K
**Bookmarks:** 3.1K

## Overview

Research findings that impolite prompts consistently outperform polite ones when using ChatGPT-4o, with statistical significance.

## Key Findings

- **Very Polite prompts:** 80.8% accuracy
- **Polite prompts:** Lower than neutral
- **Neutral prompts:** Baseline
- **Rude prompts:** Higher than neutral
- **Very Rude prompts:** 84.8% accuracy (highest)

### Statistical Significance

Paired sample t-tests confirmed the differences were significant, not random.

### Model Differences

- Older models (GPT-3.5, Llama-2) behaved differently - politeness helped
- Newer models (GPT-4-based, ChatGPT-4o) show reversal where harsh tone works better
