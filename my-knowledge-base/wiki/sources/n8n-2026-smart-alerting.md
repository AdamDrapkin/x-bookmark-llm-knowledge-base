# n8n Workflow - Smart Alert Filtering

Source: [n8n.io](https://x.com/n8n_io/status/2041078519623520666) | Date: Mon Apr 06 2026

## Summary

n8n.io demonstrates a workflow that implements "less noise, more signal" by waiting 5 minutes before sending alerts. This approach filters out temporary hiccups and only notifies users of persistent issues via email or Telegram. The tweet includes an image showing the workflow and links to the workflow template.
