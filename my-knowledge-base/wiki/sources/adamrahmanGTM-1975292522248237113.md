# 6 AI Prompts For GTM Strategy Development

**Author:** [Adam Rahman](https://x.com/AdamrahmanGTM/status/1975292522248237113)
**Date:** October 6, 2025
**Bookmarks:** 1.6K

## Overview

 Adam Rahman shares six AI prompts for developing comprehensive GTM strategy, covering market research through messaging creation.

## The Six Prompts

### 1. Deep Market Research
Tool: GPT-4 (Deep Research Mode)

Comprehensive market analysis covering:
- Company analysis (offerings, business model, geographic presence)
- Market landscape (TAM, segments, buyer personas)
- Value proposition analysis
- Competitive intelligence
- GTM challenges

### 2. TAM Mapping
Tool: Claude 4 (Extended Thinking)

Industry segmentation and market sizing:
- Industry segments with NAICS codes
- Market size and technology spend
- Adoption readiness
- Budget authority
- Decision timelines

### 3. ICP Validation
Tool: Claude 4 (Extended Thinking)

Precision-targeted ICP development:
- Demographic profiles
- Psychographic profiles
- Needs-based analysis
- Business drivers
- Lookalike profiles

### 4. Company Account Sourcing
Deep research for company databases:
- Online directories
- Scraping methods
- Niche paid databases

### 5. Targeting Keywords Generation
Industry and persona keywords:
- Database filtering keywords
- More accurate than general industry filtering

### 6. Messaging Creation
Email script variations based on previous prompts:
- Length variations
- Different offers
- Pain points
- Case studies
- Complexity levels
