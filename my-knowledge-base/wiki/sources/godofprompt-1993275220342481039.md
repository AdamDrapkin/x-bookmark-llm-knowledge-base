## Summary

This tweet promotes a thread that bridges [[Robert Greene]]'s seminal psychological framework—the *48 Laws of Power*—with modern [[prompt engineering]] techniques. The concept suggests that [[LLMs]] can serve as practical tools for applying centuries of accumulated wisdom on human behavior, influence, and power dynamics. The thread reportedly offers seven specific prompts designed to help users internalize Greene's principles, making them "sharper, harder to manipulate, and impossible to ignore." The visual branding cleverly combines Greene's iconic book cover (featuring the stark portrait and red-white color scheme) with the logos of major AI platforms ([[Gemini]], [[ChatGPT]], [[Claude]], [[Grok]]), implying these principles can be operationalized across any AI assistant. This represents a growing trend of applying philosophical and psychological frameworks through conversational AI, treating LLMs not just as information tools but as interactive systems for behavioral conditioning and strategic thinking.

- **Cross-disciplinary application**: Robert Greene's psychological principles can be systematized through [[AI prompts]] for practical, real-world application
