# Day 1 Viral Growth: 21 Videos, 390K Views, $80 from Zero

**Author:** @alexxgrowth

## Summary

@alexxgrowth documents an aggressive content experiment on a brand-new social media account, posting 21 videos across just 1.5 days without any follower base or account warmup. The results were remarkable: over 390,000 total views, a 95% approval rate on submissions (20 out of 21), organic growth to 450 followers, and $80 in earnings generated directly from content. The account focuses on [[viral content]] themes around "cheaters caught" and real cheating stories, with the bio directing followers to [[Plutus.gg]] where users can "earn while playing games."

The [[Content Rewards]] dashboard visible in the attached screenshots shows specific payout breakdowns—$14.09 for approximately 75,000 views and $11.36 for roughly 61,000 views on individual pieces of content. The @cheatergottbusted profile displays 394.1K views in the trailing 30 days across 35 posts, indicating sustained momentum beyond the initial 1.5-day posting sprint. This experiment challenges conventional wisdom about needing extended account warmup periods or established audiences before seeing significant reach.

## Key Takeaways

- A fresh account with zero followers can achieve 390K+ views within 1.5 days through high-volume posting
- 20 of 21 submissions were approved, suggesting the platform's algorithm responds positively to certain content types regardless of account age
- The $80 in earnings came directly through [[Content Rewards]] monetization, a platform-specific program
- Content theme matters significantly—"cheaters caught" and loyalty-related stories appear to generate strong engagement
- Volume strategy (21 videos in 1.5 days) can compensate for lack of established audience
- Gaming-related external links (Plutus.gg) serve as an additional monetization and engagement pathway
