## Summary

This tweet serves as a compact, opinionated resource list for [[web-designers]] and [[front-end-developers]] seeking high-quality design tools. The author frames these eight tools as "should be illegal to know"—implying they provide unfair competitive advantage through superior quality or efficiency. The list spans the complete design workflow: from finding unique [[background-textures]] and [[typography]] options, to removing backgrounds from images, generating custom imagery, creating [[bento-grid]] layouts, and converting designs across formats. The final entry—Magician for Figma—bridges the gap between design and implementation by enabling AI-powered design-to-code workflows. This curated approach cuts through the noise of countless tools available, offering a focused toolkit for rapid web development.

## Key Takeaways

- **[[grainient]]** provides grainy, textured backgrounds ideal for adding visual depth and a modern aesthetic to web projects
- **[[fontshare]]** (fontshare.com) offers a library of commercial-grade fonts, solving the licensing concerns that come with premium typography
- **[[remove-bg]]** automates the tedious task of background removal, saving hours of manual editing work
- **[[kling-ai]]** enables animation of virtually any visual element, bringing static designs to life
- **[[midjourney]]** serves as a powerful [[ai-image-generation]] tool for creating custom visuals from text prompts
- **[[bentogrids]]** provides pre-designed [[bento-grid]] layouts for organizing content in modern, visually striking patterns
- **[[magician-for-figma]]** (magicianfigma.com) bridges design-to-code workflows with AI-powered Figma-to-code conversion
- The framing as "illegal to know" reflects the design community's emphasis on discovering powerful tools before they become mainstream
