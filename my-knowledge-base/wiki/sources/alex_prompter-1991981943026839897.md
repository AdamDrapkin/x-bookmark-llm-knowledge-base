**Views:** 687
**Bookmarks:** 7

## Overview

A three-step method for learning anything 10x faster using YouTube transcripts and AI image generation.

## The Method

### Step 1: Find a value-packed YT video
Search for high-quality educational content on your topic.

### Step 2: Get transcript from ytscribe.ai
Use [ytscribe.ai](https://ytscribe.ai) to extract the transcript.

### Step 3: Use prompt in Gemini
Generate a visual cheatsheet:
> "Generate an image by turning this transcript into a concise cheatsheet with key takeaways."

## Why This Works

- Visual learning is faster than reading
- Key takeaways extracted automatically
- Cheatsheet is easy to review
