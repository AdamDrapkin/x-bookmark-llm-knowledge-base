# Stunt - AI TikTok Slideshow Cloner Tool

**Author:** @JamesEbringer
**Category:** content-creation

## Summary

Stunt is an emerging AI tool marketed to TikTok creators seeking rapid content scaling. The platform's core value proposition is straightforward: paste any viral video link, and the AI generates a recreation featuring your own character in under 60 seconds. The tool specifically targets the slideshow format that dominated TikTok's viral landscape—those text-over-image posts with trending audio that accumulated millions of views.

The landing page reveals a feature set focused on speed and scale. "Hook Clone" appears to be the core technology, allowing users to replicate successful opening sequences that captured attention. The pricing model shows "Free to sign up," suggesting a freemium approach with premium features behind a paywall. The platform positions itself as a content arbitrage tool—enabling users to capitalize on proven viral formats without the creative labor of designing slides themselves.

The example TikTok profile (@coporate.kayla) illustrates the types of content being cloned: corporate humor, productivity hacks, and relatable workplace scenarios. This profile accumulated 532.7K views on a single "unhinged corporate hacks" video, demonstrating the viral potential of this content category. The hooks shown include emotional triggers ("we're so proud of our daughter"), personal development angles ("POV: You finally locked in"), and curiosity-driven formats ("I could KISS the flight attendant who showed me this").

This represents a significant shift in the content creation landscape—from human creativity to algorithmic replication of proven formats.
