## Summary

This tweet is a [[lead-magnet]] marketing tactic promoting [[claude-opus]] as a consulting replacement. The actual prompts are not disclosed in the tweet itself—instead, the author uses a [[engagement-bait]] technique: requiring users to comment "Claude" to receive a "complete Claude Mastery guide" via direct message. The tweet makes a bold claim about AI capabilities replacing professional services but provides no evidence or examples of actual outputs.
