# OpenCreator - AI-Powered Mass Ad Production

**Author:** [el.cine](https://x.com/EHuanglu/status/1991560454104313914)
**Date:** November 20, 2025
**Views:** 11.1K
**Bookmarks:** 177

## Overview

A node-based AI canvas that generates hundreds of advertising images for any product in about 40 seconds.

## What OpenCreator Does

- Created with hundreds of nodes and prompts
- Analyzes your product image
- Generates 100s of advertising images matching your product
- Processing time: ~40 seconds
- Upload product photo, click Run

## The Technology

- Node-based workflow editor
- Multi-model AI (GPT-4o, others)
- Creative instruction prompts
- Batch generation
- Auto and manual modes

## Features

- **Product Image Input**: Upload any product photo
- **Model Selection**: Choose AI model(s)
- **Creative Instructions**: Custom prompt instructions
- **Batch Generation**: Create multiple variations
- **Results**: Download generated images
- **Edit/Share/Shop**: Various output options
