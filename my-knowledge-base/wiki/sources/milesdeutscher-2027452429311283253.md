## Summary

Perplexity has launched a new "Computer tool" that directly targets the OpenClaw market, positioning itself as a superior alternative for AI-driven browser automation and agentic workflows. Tech commentator and AI tool reviewer @milesdeutscher, who has built a substantial following analyzing productivity tools and AI systems, was notably impressed by the release—calling it "the most powerful agentic system on the market right now." The comparison to OpenClaw is significant because OpenClaw has established itself as a leading solution for enabling AI agents to interact with web browsers and desktop applications through natural language commands. Perplexity's entry into this space with claims of easier setup, enhanced security, and expanded functionality represents a meaningful competitive development. Miles Deutscher has curated a set of 10 optimized "mega prompts" specifically designed for this tool, which he shared publicly for users to copy and paste, essentially providing a starting framework for getting maximum value from Perplexity's Computer tool immediately upon release.
