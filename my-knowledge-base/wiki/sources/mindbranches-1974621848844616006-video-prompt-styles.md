## Summary

This tweet from MindBranches provides a comprehensive guide to styles that can be used in AI video generation prompts. The image contains an extensive list organized into categories:

- **Cinematic & Professional**: Film/movie styles, TV shows, commercials, music videos
- **Surveillance & Monitoring**: CCTV, body cams, drones, security footage
- **Personal & Casual**: iPhone video, vlogs, GoPro, webcam
- **Vintage & Analog**: 8mm/16mm/35mm film, VHS, Betamax
- **Online & Social Media**: TikTok, YouTube, Twitch, memes
- **Experimental & Artistic**: Camera effects, slow motion, animation
- **Institutional**: Educational, corporate

This is a practical reference for anyone generating AI videos who wants specific stylistic outputs.
