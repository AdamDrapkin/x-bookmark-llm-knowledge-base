# Conversational Prompting

**Author:** [Machina](https://x.com/EXM7777/status/1987203971086450938)
**Date:** November 8, 2025
**Views:** 17.4K
**Bookmarks:** 356

## Overview

A prompting technique that completely changes how AI interacts with you.

## The Technique

Add this line to every prompt:
> "Ask one question at a time, keep it conversational, and let me lead the discussion instead of interviewing me"

## What Happens

- You end up doing the thinking
- AI just guides the process
- Output quality goes up 10x

## Why It Works

- Prevents AI from being an interrogator
- Keeps you in control of the conversation
- AI becomes a thought partner, not a quiz master
