## Summary

Jaynit Makwana announced the launch of [[ribbi]], described as the "most powerful all-in-one AI creative agent of 2026." The platform solves a common pain point for content creators by accepting a single input—drop one link—and automatically transforming it into multiple content formats including [[videos]], [[carousels]], [[posters]], and [[dubbed-content]].

Beyond creation, Ribbi handles the entire [[content-workflow]]: it automatically schedules posts, publishes across platforms, and tracks performance metrics. This end-to-end automation represents a shift from tools that only assist with one stage of content production to platforms that manage the complete lifecycle.

The announcement positions Ribbi as a [[time-saving-tool]] for creators, agencies, and brands looking to maximize the value of each piece of content they produce. The "20 posts from one" framing suggests significant content multiplication—taking a single source (likely a blog post, article, or long-form content) and generating enough derivative content to maintain an active posting schedule.
