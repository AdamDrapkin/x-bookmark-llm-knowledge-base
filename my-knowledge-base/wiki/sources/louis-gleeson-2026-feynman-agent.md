Source: [Louis Gleeson](https://x.com/aigleeson/status/2041073339616387468) | Date posted: 2026-04-06

## Summary

Feynman is an open source AI research agent that accomplishes in seconds what typically takes PhDs hours. Users type a topic, and Feynman searches academic papers, synthesizes findings, verifies every claim against real sources, and delivers a cited research brief.
