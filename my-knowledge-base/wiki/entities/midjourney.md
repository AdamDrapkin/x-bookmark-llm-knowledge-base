# Midjourney

**Type:** Product/Tool
**Known For:** An AI-powered image generation tool that creates images from text descriptions, widely used by artists, designers, and creative professionals for generating visual content.

## Overview

Midjourney is an independent research lab that developed an AI system capable of generating images from natural language prompts. Launched in beta in 2022, the tool utilizes deep learning models to interpret text descriptions and produce detailed, stylized images. Users interact with the system through Discord or its web interface, specifying parameters like aspect ratio, style, and quality to guide the image generation process. The platform has gained significant popularity among digital artists, content creators, and businesses for rapid prototyping and creative exploration. Midjourney is an AI image generation tool that creates visual artwork from text prompts, operated as a subscription service through Discord and web platforms.
