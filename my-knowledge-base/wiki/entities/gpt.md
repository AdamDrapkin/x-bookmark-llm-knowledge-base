# Gpt

**Type:** Product/Tool
**Known For:** A series of large language models developed by OpenAI that use deep learning to generate human-like text, powering conversational AI applications like ChatGPT.

## Overview

GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer) refers to a family of autoregressive language models that use transformer architecture to understand and generate natural language. Developed by OpenAI, the series has progressed through multiple iterations, with GPT-3 (2020) and GPT-4 (2023) being among the most prominent versions. These models are trained on vast datasets of text from the internet, enabling them to perform a wide range of language tasks including text completion, summarization, translation, and conversational interaction. GPT models represent a significant advancement in natural language processing, enabling machines to understand and generate human-like text at scale.
