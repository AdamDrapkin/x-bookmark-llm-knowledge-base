# Andrew Ng

**Andrew Ng** is a leading AI researcher, entrepreneur, and thought leader in the artificial intelligence space. He is the founder of Google Brain, co-founder of Coursera, and leading voice in AI education and implementation.

## Key Contributions

- **Google Brain** - Founded Google's deep learning research team
- **Coursera** - democratized AI education globally
- **AI Framework** - Developed the Agentic AI framework for building AI agent businesses

## The Agentic AI Framework

Ng's framework for AI agent businesses covers six stages:

1. **Plan** - Define the problem and solution architecture
2. **Build** - Develop the AI agent
3. **Deploy** - Release to production
4. **Price** - Set value-based pricing
5. **Sell** - Acquire customers
6. **Scale** - Grow operations
