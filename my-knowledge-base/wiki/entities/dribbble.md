# Dribbble

**Type:** Product/Tool/Company/Person
**Known For:** Online portfolio community for designers to showcase and discover creative work

## Overview

Dribbble is a web-based design community platform founded in 2009 by Dan Cederholm and Rich Thornett. It serves as a global showcase for designers to share, discover, and connect through visual design work including UI/UX designs, illustrations, animations, and branding projects. The platform functions as both a portfolio hosting service and a networking hub for creative professionals.
