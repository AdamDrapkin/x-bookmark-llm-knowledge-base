# Andrej Karpathy

**Type:** Person
**Known For:** AI researcher and educator known for pioneering Stanford's deep learning course and former Director of AI at Tesla; widely recognized for making deep learning accessible through his educational content and open-source work.

## Overview

Andrej Karpathy is a Slovak-Canadian computer scientist specializing in deep learning and computer vision. He earned his PhD from Stanford University under Geoffrey Hinton, where he taught the popular CS231n course on convolutional neural networks. Karpathy previously worked as a research scientist at OpenAI and served as Director of AI at Tesla, where he led the development of Autopilot's computer vision systems. He currently runs educational platforms including deeplearning.ai and maintains a significant presence on YouTube and Twitter, where he shares technical insights and tutorials on AI. Andrej Karpathy is a leading AI researcher and educator who has significantly shaped the field through his academic contributions at Stanford and Tesla, as well as his influential online courses that have trained millions of developers and researchers worldwide.
