# Anthropic

**Type:** AI Company
**Founded:** 2021
**Headquarters:** San Francisco, California
**Known For:** Claude AI assistant, constitutional AI, prompt engineering research

## Overview

Anthropic is an AI safety and research company founded by former OpenAI researchers. They created Claude, an AI assistant focused on being helpful, harmless, and honest. Anthropic is known for their work on constitutional AI - training AI systems to follow human values through explicit behavioral guidelines.

## Key Products

- **Claude** — AI assistant for complex reasoning and task execution
- **Constitutional AI** — Framework for aligning AI systems with human values
- **Prompt Library** — Internal prompting techniques now public
