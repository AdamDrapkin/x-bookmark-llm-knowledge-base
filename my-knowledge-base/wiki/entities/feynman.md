# Feynman (AI Research Agent)

**Feynman** is an open source AI research agent designed to automate academic research. It accomplishes in seconds what traditionally takes researchers hours - searching papers, synthesizing findings, verifying claims, and producing cited research briefs.

## Key Capabilities

- **Literature Search** - Searches academic papers on any topic
- **Synthesis** - Summarizes findings from multiple sources
- **Claim Verification** - Verifies every assertion against real sources
- **Report Generation** - Produces formatted, cited research briefs

## How It Works

Users type a research topic, and Feynman:
1. Searches relevant academic papers
2. Identifies key findings and methodologies
3. Verifies claims against source materials
4. Synthesizes a coherent research brief with citations

## Not a Chatbot

Feynman is not a general chatbot - it's a specialized research tool focused on academic literature synthesis and verification.
