# Competitive Intelligence

**Category:** Method
**Definition:** The systematic process of gathering, analyzing, and applying information about competitors, market trends, and business environments to enhance strategic decision-making.

## Overview

Competitive intelligence involves monitoring and analyzing competitor activities, market dynamics, and industry developments to identify opportunities and threats. It employs both traditional research methods and modern AI-powered tools to transform raw data into actionable strategic insights. The practice draws parallels to strategic thinking in competitive games, where understanding your opponent's mindset provides decisive advantages.
