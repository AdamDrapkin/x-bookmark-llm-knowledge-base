# Prompt Engineering

**Prompt Engineering** is the discipline of crafting effective instructions (prompts) for AI models to achieve desired outputs. It involves understanding how AI models interpret input and structuring prompts to maximize the quality and relevance of responses.

## Key Techniques

### Zero-shot Prompting
Giving the model a task without examples, relying on its general knowledge.

### Few-shot Prompting
Providing a few examples within the prompt to guide the model's output style.

### Chain-of-Thought (CoT)
Prompting the model to show its reasoning steps, improving performance on complex tasks.

### Role-playing
Assigning the AI a specific persona or role to influence its responses.

## Applications

- **Content Generation** - Writing, summarization, translation
- **Data Extraction** - Pulling structured data from unstructured text
- **Code Assistance** - Writing, debugging, explaining code
- **Design** - Generating design specs and specifications
