# Knowledge Management

**Category:** General
**Definition:** The discipline and set of practices for capturing, organizing, storing, and retrieving information and insights to support learning, decision‑making, and innovation.

## Overview

Knowledge management (KM) encompasses the strategies, tools, and workflows that enable individuals and organizations to create, share, and apply knowledge effectively. Recent advances have introduced AI‑powered personal knowledge bases—such as Andrej Karpathy’s “second brain” system—that leverage large language models (LLMs) to automatically tag, link, and retrieve notes and research materials. Complementary open‑source utilities like Field Theory provide command‑line interfaces to sync external sources (e.g., X bookmarks) locally, further enriching the personal knowledge ecosystem.
