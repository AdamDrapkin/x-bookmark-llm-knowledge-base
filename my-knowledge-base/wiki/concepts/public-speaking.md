# Public Speaking

**Category:** Technique
**Definition:** The art of effectively conveying ideas, information, and emotions to an audience in a clear, engaging, and persuasive manner.

## Overview

Public speaking is a fundamental skill for communicating ideas and influencing audiences. A landmark MIT lecture by Patrick Winston on communication and public speaking has gained over 18 million views, offering practical insights into what makes speakers effective. The lecture presents provocative ideas about communication strategies.
