# Divergent Thinking

**Category:** Technique
**Definition:** A creative problem-solving approach that explores multiple possible solutions by expanding thinking in many directions, generating a wide range of ideas without immediate judgment or evaluation.

## Overview

Divergent thinking is a cognitive process used to generate creative ideas by exploring many possible solutions or interpretations. It involves brainstorming, free association, and exploring unconventional paths to expand the solution space before converging on the best option.
