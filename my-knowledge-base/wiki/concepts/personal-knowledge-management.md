# Personal Knowledge Management

**Category:** Method
**Definition:** A systematic approach to collecting, organizing, storing, and retrieving information for personal use and productivity.

## Overview

Personal Knowledge Management (PKM) encompasses digital and analog systems used to capture, organize, and leverage accumulated knowledge. Modern PKM increasingly integrates AI tools to automate information retrieval and connection-making. Effective PKM systems balance structure with flexibility, enabling users to transform scattered information into actionable insights.
