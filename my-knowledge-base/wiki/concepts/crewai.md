# Crewai

**Category:** Tool
**Definition:** An open‑source framework for building, deploying, and orchestrating multi‑agent AI systems that collaborate to accomplish complex tasks.

## Overview

Crewai provides a flexible, modular environment for developers to define agents with specific roles, equip them with tools, and coordinate their actions through a hierarchical task pipeline. It abstracts low‑level LLM interaction, enabling seamless integration with popular language models and external APIs while offering built‑in observability and error‑handling features.
