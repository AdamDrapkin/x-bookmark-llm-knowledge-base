# Frameworks

**Category:** General
**Definition:** A reusable set of conventions, guidelines, and reusable components that provide structure for building software or solving problems within a specific domain.

## Overview

Frameworks establish a foundation of reusable patterns, tools, and methodologies that developers can leverage to build applications more efficiently. Rather than starting from scratch, frameworks provide pre-built components and standard approaches that handle common concerns, allowing builders to focus on domain-specific problems. They serve as scaffolds that shape how solutions are constructed, promoting consistency and reducing decision fatigue across projects. Frameworks accelerate development by providing structured foundations of reusable conventions, tools, and patterns that guide problem-solving and application construction.
