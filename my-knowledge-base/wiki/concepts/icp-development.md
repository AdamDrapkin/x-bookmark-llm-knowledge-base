# ICP Development

**ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) Development** is the process of defining the characteristics of your perfect customer. This drives targeting, messaging, and sales strategy.

## ICP Dimensions

- **Firmographics** — Company size, industry, tech stack
- **Demographics** — Role, team size, budget authority
- **Behavioral** — Purchase patterns, content consumption
- **Psychographic** — Goals, challenges, values

## AIApplications

- Prompt-based ICP generation
- Validation against real company data
- Scoring and prioritization
