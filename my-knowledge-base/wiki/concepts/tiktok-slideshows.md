# Tiktok Slideshows

**Category:** Technique
**Definition:** A TikTok slideshow is a short video composed of a series of static images combined with audio, transitions, text overlays, and effects, typically created using TikTok’s native slideshow creator or external editing tools.

## Overview

TikTok slideshows enable creators to present multiple images in a single video clip, syncing them with music and optional text overlays. The platform’s built‑in slideshow tool lets users select up to 35 images, apply transition effects, adjust timing, and add filters before posting. These videos are widely used for storytelling, product showcases, tutorials, and before‑and‑after comparisons. TikTok slideshows offer a straightforward method for turning a sequence of images into engaging, music‑backed micro‑videos on the platform.
