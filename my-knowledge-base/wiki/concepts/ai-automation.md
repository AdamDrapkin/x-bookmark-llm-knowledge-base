# Ai Automation

**Category:** Tool
**Definition:** The use of artificial intelligence systems to perform tasks or processes with minimal human intervention.

## Overview

AI automation involves deploying machine learning models, natural language processing, and other AI technologies to execute repetitive or complex tasks that traditionally require human input. This technology enables businesses to streamline operations, reduce errors, and scale processes efficiently across various domains including customer service, data analysis, and decision-making. It represents the convergence of automation capabilities with intelligent, adaptive systems. AI automation leverages intelligent algorithms to execute tasks autonomously, enhancing efficiency and scalability across business operations.
