# Saas

**Category:** General
**Definition:** Software as a Service (SaaS) is a cloud‑based delivery model where software is licensed on a subscription basis, hosted by the provider, and accessed via the internet.

## Overview

SaaS companies increasingly rely on distribution strategies that prioritize rapid user acquisition over feature depth, especially when offering AI‑generated products. Greg Isenberg’s masterclass highlights seven proven distribution loops—ranging from product‑led growth to community‑driven virality—that can be coupled with value‑based pricing such as a $19.99/month anchor to drive sustainable revenue milestones. This approach challenges the traditional “build‑first‑sell‑later” mindset, emphasizing that distribution and price positioning are critical levers for growth in the SaaS ecosystem.
