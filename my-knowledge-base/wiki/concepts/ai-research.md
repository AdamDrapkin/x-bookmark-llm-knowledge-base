# Ai Research

**Category:** General
**Definition:** The systematic use of artificial intelligence tools and methodologies for conducting research, information gathering, and knowledge synthesis.

## Overview

AI research encompasses various approaches for leveraging artificial intelligence systems to enhance information discovery, analysis, and knowledge creation. Practitioners utilize AI-powered search engines, specialized prompts, and automated research tools to accelerate the research process. The field includes both using AI as a research instrument and sharing resources that optimize AI-assisted investigation.
