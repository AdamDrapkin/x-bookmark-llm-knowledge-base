# Garry Tan

**Category:** General
**Definition:** Garry Tan is the CEO of Y Combinator and the creator of gstack, an open‑source toolkit that expands the capabilities of the AI coding assistant Claude Code.

## Overview

Garry Tan is a leading figure in the startup world, steering Y Combinator as its chief executive while also contributing directly to the developer community through open‑source projects. He released **gstack**, his personal toolkit, to transform Claude Code from a simple chatbot into a more powerful development environment. This initiative underscores his commitment to making advanced AI tools accessible and customizable for developers everywhere.
