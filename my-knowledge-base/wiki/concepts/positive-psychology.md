# Positive Psychology

**Category:** General
**Definition:** The scientific study of human flourishing, well-being, and optimal functioning, focusing on strengths, virtues, and factors that enable individuals and communities to thrive.

## Overview

Positive psychology is an interdisciplinary field that explores what makes life worth living and how people can achieve fulfillment. It shift from traditional psychology's focus on pathology and deficits to examining positive emotions, engagement, relationships, meaning, and accomplishment. The field employs empirical research methods to understand and promote human flourishing.
