# Context Engineering

**Category:** Technique
**Definition:** The practice of structuring, filtering, and presenting contextual information to maximize AI response quality and relevance.

## Overview

Context Engineering involves carefully designing the input context provided to AI systems, including how information is organized, prioritized, and formatted within prompts. It focuses on optimizing the signal-to-noise ratio in context windows to help AI models produce more accurate, relevant, and useful outputs. This technique has become increasingly important as AI systems are applied to complex, real-world tasks requiring precise understanding of user intent and situational requirements.
