# Monitoring

**Category:** Operations / Observability
**Purpose:** Track systems, processes, and metrics to ensure health and performance

## Overview

Monitoring involves the continuous observation of systems, processes, or metrics to ensure they function correctly. In technology and business contexts, monitoring is essential for maintaining uptime, detecting issues early, and optimizing performance.

## Types of Monitoring

- **Infrastructure Monitoring** — Servers, networks, cloud resources
- **Application Monitoring** — Performance, errors, user behavior
- **Business Metrics** — KPIs, conversion rates, revenue
- **Security Monitoring** — Threat detection, access logs
