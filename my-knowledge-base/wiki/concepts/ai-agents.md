# Ai Agents

**Category:** Tool
**Definition:** Autonomous software programs that use artificial intelligence to execute tasks, make decisions, and interact with other systems on behalf of users.

## Overview

AI agents are specialized autonomous programs that extend beyond simple chatbots to perform complex, multi-step tasks. They leverage frameworks like gstack and Paperclip to transform basic AI capabilities into powerful tools capable of writing viral content, managing workflows, and executing specialized functions. These agents are increasingly accessible through open-source toolkits, enabling developers and startups to build custom autonomous solutions.
