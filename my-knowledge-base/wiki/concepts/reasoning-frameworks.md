# Reasoning Frameworks

**Reasoning Frameworks** are structured approaches to analysis and problem-solving. In AI prompting, these are established methodologies that guide AI thinking for better outputs.

## Key Frameworks

### Chain-of-Thought (CoT)
Show reasoning steps for complex problems.

### Game Theory
Strategic decision-making frameworks.

### Premortem
Imagine failure and work backward to prevent it.

### Feynman Technique
Explain simply to verify understanding.

### First Principles
Break down to fundamentals.
