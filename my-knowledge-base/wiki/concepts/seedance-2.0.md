# Seedance 2.0

**Category:** Tool
**Definition:** An advanced AI-driven video synthesis platform that expands on the original Seedance model, providing higher visual fidelity, real‑time editing capabilities, and seamless integration with other AI filmmaking tools.

## Overview

Seedance 2.0 is rumored to be the next iteration of the Seedance AI video engine, first highlighted by speculative posts from observers such as Linus Ekenstam in early 2026. The platform is expected to tighten its partnership with Flova AI, enabling creators to blend narrative, visual style, and audio cues in a unified workflow. By offering more granular control over motion, lighting, and scene composition, Seedance 2.0 aims to lower the barrier for AI‑enhanced filmmaking.
