# TAM Analysis

**TAM (Total Addressable Market) Analysis** is the process of calculating the total market opportunity for a product or service. It represents the maximum revenue potential if 100% market share was achieved.

## TAM Components

- **TAM** — Total Addressable Market (entire market)
- **SAM** — Serviceable Available Market (segment you can target)
- **SOM** — Serviceable Obtainable Market (realisticCapture)

## AI Applications

- Prompt-based market sizing
- Competitive market analysis
- Growth projection modeling
