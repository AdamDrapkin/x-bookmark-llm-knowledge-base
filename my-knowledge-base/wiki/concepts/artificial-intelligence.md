# Artificial Intelligence

**Category:** General
**Definition:** Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the field of computer science devoted to creating machines and software that can perform tasks requiring human cognition, such as learning, reasoning, perception, and decision‑making.

## Overview

Artificial Intelligence aims to replicate aspects of human intelligence in machines, allowing them to acquire knowledge from data, adapt to new information, and execute tasks autonomously. It encompasses a variety of approaches—including rule‑based systems, statistical learning, and neural networks—that together enable applications ranging from voice assistants to autonomous vehicles. Ongoing research focuses on improving performance, safety, and ethical alignment of AI technologies.
