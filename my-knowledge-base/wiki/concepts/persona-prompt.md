# Persona Prompt

**Category:** Technique
**Definition:** A persona prompt is a generative instruction that assigns a specific role, expertise, or character to an AI model, shaping its language, reasoning style, and depth of response.

## Overview

Persona prompts embed a chosen identity—real or fictional—into the model’s instruction set, causing it to adopt the perspective, vocabulary, and analytical approach typical of that role. This framing can make outputs more engaging, context‑appropriate, and aligned with the desired tone, effectively offering a free alternative to formal education that often emphasizes memorization over critical thinking. By tailoring the persona to the task, users can elicit richer, more nuanced answers without additional cost.
