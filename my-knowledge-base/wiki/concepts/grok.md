# Grok

**Grok** is xAI's AI assistant, built by the same team behind X (Twitter). It's known for witty, personality-driven responses and access to real-time information.

## Key Capabilities

- **Real-time Knowledge** — Access to current X content
- **Witty Personality** — Humorous, unfiltered responses
- **Advanced Reasoning** — Grok 4.1 models with strong reasoning
- **Writing Assistant** — Creative and analytical writing
