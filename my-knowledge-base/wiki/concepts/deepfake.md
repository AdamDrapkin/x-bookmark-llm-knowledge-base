# Deepfake

**Category:** Technique
**Definition:** A deepfake is AI‑generated synthetic media—typically video or audio—in which a person's likeness or voice is convincingly superimposed onto another individual or content using deep learning models.

## Overview

Deepfakes employ generative neural networks to manipulate visual and audio elements, enabling realistic face swapping, lip‑syncing, expression transfer, and voice synthesis. Originally confined to research labs, the technology has rapidly moved to consumer‑grade applications, dramatically lowering the expertise needed to produce convincing fake media. Recent tools such as Deep‑Live‑Cam illustrate the shift toward real‑time, accessible deepfake creation, heightening concerns about privacy, misinformation, and identity abuse.
