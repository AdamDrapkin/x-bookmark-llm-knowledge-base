# AI Business

An **AI Business** is any business model that leverages AI capabilities to provide value to customers. This includes businesses built on AI agents, AI-powered services, and "wrapper" businesses that simplify complex AI tools for end users.

## Business Models

### Agent-as-a-Service
AI agents offered as hosted services that customers can invoke via API or interface.

### AI Wrapper
A business that takes an existing AI tool with a learning curve and creates a simplified interface or pre-configured workflows for users who don't want to learn prompting.

### Vertical AI
AI solutions tailored for specific industries or use cases.

## Key Considerations

- **Pricing** - Value-based pricing for AI capabilities
- **Scalability** - How to grow the business efficiently
- **Differentiation** - What makes your AI offering unique
- **Integration** - How your AI fits into customer workflows
