# Alerting

**Category:** Automation / Notification
**Purpose:** Notify users of important events or conditions

## Overview

Alerting refers to systems that notify users when specific events occur or thresholds are met. In workflow automation, alerting is crucial for keeping humans informed about automated processes without requiring constant monitoring.

## Types of Alerts

- **Threshold Alerts** — Triggered when a metric exceeds or falls below a value
- **Event Alerts** — Triggered when specific events occur (new lead, payment, etc.)
- **Schedule Alerts** — Time-based reminders and notifications
- **Error Alerts** — Notifications when automation workflows fail
