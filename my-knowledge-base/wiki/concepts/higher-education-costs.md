# Higher Education Costs

**Category:** General
**Definition:** The total financial investment required for post-secondary academic programs, including tuition, fees, housing, materials, and associated living expenses.

## Overview

Higher education costs have risen dramatically over the past several decades, often outpacing inflation and wage growth. These expenses present significant barriers to access for many students and families. Understanding the full scope of costs and available financial resources is essential for making informed academic and financial decisions.
