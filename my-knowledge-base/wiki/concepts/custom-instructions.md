# Custom Instructions

**Category:** Technique
**Definition:** Persistent directives that define AI behavior, preferences, and constraints across multiple conversations without repetition.

## Overview

Custom Instructions allow users to establish consistent parameters, tone guidelines, and task-specific rules that persist throughout all interactions with an AI system. This technique solves context window overload by embedding essential preferences and requirements directly into the session, eliminating the need to re-explain priorities in every new conversation.
