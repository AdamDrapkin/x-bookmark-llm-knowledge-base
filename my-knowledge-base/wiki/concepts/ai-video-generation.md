# Ai Video Generation

**Category:** General
**Definition:** The process of creating video content using artificial intelligence systems that generate, manipulate, or enhance visual media through machine learning models.

## Overview

AI video generation refers to the technological capability of producing video content through AI models that can synthesize, animate, or modify visual sequences. This field has evolved rapidly with advances in diffusion models and transformer architectures, enabling creators to generate video from text prompts, images, or existing video footage. The technology spans from professional production workflows to casual creative experimentation.
