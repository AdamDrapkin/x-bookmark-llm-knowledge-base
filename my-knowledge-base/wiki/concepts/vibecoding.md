# Vibecoding

**Category:** Technique
**Definition:** A development approach that relies on describing desired functionality in natural language and leveraging AI to generate code, emphasizing high-level intent over explicit implementation details.

## Overview

Vibecoding is a paradigm where developers articulate what they want a program to accomplish, rather than how to build it, and use AI models to translate those descriptions into functional code. The method relies heavily on the developer's ability to provide clear, high-level goals and a robust vocabulary for UI components, which directly influences the quality of the generated front‑end code. By iteratively refining prompts based on AI feedback, developers can rapidly prototype and iterate on software.
