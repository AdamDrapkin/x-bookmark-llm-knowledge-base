# Ai Prompts

**Category:** Tool
**Definition:** Structured text inputs used to guide artificial intelligence systems, particularly large language models, toward desired outputs or behaviors.

## Overview

Ai prompts are text-based instructions or queries that communicate user intent to AI systems. They serve as the primary interface between human goals and machine responses, enabling users to leverage AI capabilities across diverse tasks from creative writing to technical problem-solving. The effectiveness of AI prompts varies based on clarity, specificity, and structural design.
