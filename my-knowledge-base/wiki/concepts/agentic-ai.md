# Agentic AI

**Agentic AI** refers to AI systems capable of planning, executing, and adapting multi-step tasks autonomously without constant human intervention. Unlike traditional AI models that respond to single prompts, agentic AI can break down complex goals into sub-tasks, iterate on solutions, and handle edge cases.

## Key Characteristics

- **Autonomous Planning**: Can decompose complex goals into actionable steps
- **Tool Use**: Can interact with external systems, APIs, and tools
- **Iteration**: Can refine outputs based on feedback
- **Persistence**: Maintains context across multiple interactions

## Framework: Plan, Build, Deploy, Price, Sell, Scale

Andrew Ng's agentic AI framework provides a structured approach to building AI agent businesses:

1. **Plan** - Define the problem and solution architecture
2. **Build** - Develop the AI agent with appropriate capabilities
3. **Deploy** - Release to production with monitoring
4. **Price** - Set appropriate pricing for the value provided
5. **Sell** - Market and acquire customers
6. **Scale** - Grow the business operations
