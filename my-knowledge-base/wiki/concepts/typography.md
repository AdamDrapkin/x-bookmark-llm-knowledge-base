# Typography

**Category:** General
**Definition:** Typography is the art and technique of arranging type to make written language legible, readable, and visually appealing.

## Overview

Typography governs how text is styled, spaced, and positioned to convey meaning and evoke mood. It is a fundamental element of visual communication across print, digital media, and branding, influencing user experience and brand perception.
