# Conversion Optimization

**Conversion Optimization** is the practice of maximizing desired outcomes. In marketing, it means maximizing sales/conversions. In AI prompting, it means getting the best possible outputs.

## Marketing Applications

- **A/B Testing** — Comparing messaging variants
- **Landing Pages** — Optimized for action
- **Email Sequences** — Nurturing to conversion
- **Ad Copy** — Click-through optimization

## AI Prompting Applications

- Specific over generic prompts
- Tone-appropriate instructions
- Clear output format specifications
