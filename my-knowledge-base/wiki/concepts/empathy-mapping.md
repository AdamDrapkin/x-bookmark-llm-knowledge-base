# Empathy Mapping

**Category:** Technique
**Definition:** A visual collaboration tool used to understand and summarize user needs, motivations, thoughts, feelings, and behaviors.

## Overview

Empathy mapping is a collaborative exercise typically conducted with cross-functional teams to gain deeper insight into target users or stakeholders. The tool captures what users say, think, do, feel, and their pain points versus gains, providing a holistic view of the user experience. It is widely used in design thinking, user research synthesis, and product development to foster user-centered decision making.
