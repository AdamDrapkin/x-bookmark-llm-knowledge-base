# Research Automation

**Research Automation** refers to the use of AI systems to automate various stages of the research process. This includes literature search, paper synthesis, claim verification, and report generation. What previously took PhDs hours can now be accomplished in seconds.

## Key Capabilities

- **Literature Search** - Finding relevant academic papers across databases
- **Synthesis** - Summarizing key findings from multiple sources
- **Claim Verification** - Checking assertions against source materials
- **Report Generation** - Producing formatted, cited research briefs

## Tools

- [[feynman]] - Open source AI research agent
- Semantic Scholar
- Elicit
- Consensus

## Benefits

- Dramatically reduces research time
- Makes academic literature more accessible
- Enables rapid exploration of new topics
- Supports evidence-based decision making
