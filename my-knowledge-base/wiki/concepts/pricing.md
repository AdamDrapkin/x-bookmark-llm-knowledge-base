# Pricing

**Category:** Business Strategy
**Purpose:** Determine the monetary value of products or services

## Overview

Pricing refers to the process of setting the monetary value at which products or services are sold. It is a critical component of business strategy that directly impacts revenue, perceived value, and market positioning.

## Common Pricing Models

- **Freemium** — Free basic version with paid premium features
- **Subscription** — Recurring payments (monthly/annual)
- **Usage-Based** — Pay for what you use
- **One-Time** — Single purchase price
- **Tiered** — Multiple price points with different feature sets
