# Lateral Thinking

**Category:** Technique
**Definition:** A problem-solving approach that involves breaking away from conventional or logical thought patterns to generate creative solutions through indirect and unexpected methods.

## Overview

Lateral thinking, pioneered by Edward de Bono, challenges the assumption that problems must be solved through sequential logical steps. Rather than moving directly toward a solution, this approach encourages thinkers to reframe problems, explore alternative perspectives, and challenge established assumptions. The technique is particularly valuable when conventional problem-solving methods have failed to produce viable solutions.
