# Claude.Md

**Category:** Method
**Definition:** A markdown-based personal knowledge management approach that leverages Claude AI for organizing, retrieving, and augmenting notes and documentation.

## Overview

Claude.Md refers to a workflow methodology where individuals use Claude AI to manage their digital notes, projects, and knowledge bases stored in markdown (.md) files. Inspired by Andrej Karpathy's "second brain" concept, this approach treats markdown files as the primary interface for AI-assisted knowledge management. The system enables seamless interaction between human-authored notes and AI-generated insights, creating an expanding personal knowledge ecosystem.
