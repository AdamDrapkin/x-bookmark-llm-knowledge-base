# Tesla

**Category:** Technique
**Definition:** A problem-solving methodology inspired by Elon Musk's approach to innovation, characterized by first principles thinking and radical simplification of complex problems.

## Overview

The Tesla technique draws from the reasoning framework Elon Musk employs when developing breakthrough technologies at Tesla and SpaceX. It involves breaking down complex challenges into their most fundamental components, then reconstructing solutions from first principles without relying on conventional assumptions or industry norms.
