# Minimax

**Category:** Technique
**Definition:** A decision-making strategy in game theory and artificial intelligence that aims to minimize the maximum possible loss by anticipating an opponent's optimal moves.

## Overview

Minimax is an algorithmic technique used primarily in two-player competitive games where one player's gain is another's loss. The algorithm evaluates possible future moves by assuming both players will act optimally—maximizing their own advantage while minimizing their opponent's. This creates a worst-case scenario analysis where a player chooses the action that leads to the best outcome even when the opponent makes the best possible counter-move. The concept originated in combinatorial game theory and became foundational in early AI research for game-playing systems.
