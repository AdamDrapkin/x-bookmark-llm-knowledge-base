# Neuralink

**Category:** Tool
**Definition:** A neurotechnology company developing brain-computer interfaces (BCIs) that enable direct communication between the human brain and electronic devices.

## Overview

Neuralink is a neurotechnology company founded by Elon Musk in 2016 with the goal of creating implantable brain-computer interfaces. The company's primary focus is on developing flexible, thread-based electrodes that can be implanted into the brain to treat neurological conditions and eventually enhance human capabilities. Their technology aims to help people with paralysis, neurological disorders, and other conditions while exploring possibilities for human-AI symbiosis.
