# Copywriting

**Copywriting** is the practice of writing persuasive text designed to drive reader action. In the AI context, it refers to prompting techniques for generating sales-oriented marketing content.

## Key Elements

- **Headlines** — Attention-grabbing openings
- **Benefits Over Features** — Focusing on value to reader
- **Call to Action** — Clear next steps
- **Urgency** — Creating desire to act now

## AI Applications

- [[prompt-engineering]] techniques for sales copy
- Role-based prompts (Mad Men era copywriter)
- Tone-specific prompting (direct, blunt)
