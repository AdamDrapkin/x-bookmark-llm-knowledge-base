# AI Ad Generation

**AI Ad Generation** is the practice of using AI tools to automatically create advertising imagery at scale. Modern tools can generate hundreds of product marketing images from a single input photo.

## Key Capabilities

- **Batch Generation** — Hundreds of variants at once
- **Style Variations** — Different contexts and settings
- **Model Integration** — Product worn by models
- **Speed** — Seconds vs days for traditional shoots

## Leading Tools

- **OpenCreator** — Node-based batch generation
- **Midjourney** — Creative ad imagery
- **DALL-E** — Product visualization
