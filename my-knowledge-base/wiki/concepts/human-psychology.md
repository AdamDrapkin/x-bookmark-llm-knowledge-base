# Human Psychology

**Category:** General
**Definition:** The scientific study of the mind, emotions, and behavior of individuals and groups.

## Overview

Human psychology examines how people think, feel, and act in various situations. It encompasses the study of mental processes, emotional responses, and behavioral patterns that shape human experience. This field draws upon research from neuroscience, sociology, and philosophy to understand what motivates and influences human actions. Human psychology is the study of the mind and behavior that seeks to explain, predict, and modify human thoughts, emotions, and actions.
