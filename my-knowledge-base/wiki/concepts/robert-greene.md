# Robert Greene

**Category:** General
**Definition:** An American author known for books about power, strategy, seduction, and mastery, whose works synthesize wisdom from historical figures and philosophical traditions.

## Overview

Robert Greene is an American author best known for his series of books examining power dynamics, strategy, and human nature. His work draws extensively from historical examples, philosophical traditions, and psychological insights to provide readers with frameworks for understanding and navigating social situations. His books have become influential in fields ranging from business to entertainment to personal development. Greene synthesizes historical wisdom and psychological insight into practical frameworks for understanding power dynamics and personal effectiveness.
