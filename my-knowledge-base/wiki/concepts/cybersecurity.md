# Cybersecurity

**Category:** General
**Definition:** The practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks, unauthorized access, and data breaches.

## Overview

Cybersecurity encompasses the technologies, processes, and practices designed to safeguard computers, servers, mobile devices, electronic systems, networks, and data from malicious attacks. As digital infrastructure becomes increasingly integral to daily life and business operations, the field addresses threats ranging from individual hacking attempts to sophisticated state-sponsored cyber operations.
