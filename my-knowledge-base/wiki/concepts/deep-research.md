# Deep Research

**Deep Research** is AI-powered comprehensive research that goes beyond surface-level search. It uses specialized prompting to synthesize original insights from multiple sources.

## Key Principles

### 1. Prioritize Sources
Tell AI exactly where to look first - don't let it wander.

### 2. Map a Protocol
Give clear research sequence instead of free-form wandering.

### 3. Set Hard Boundaries
Narrow scope = deeper insights, not generic overviews.

### 4. Define Output Format
Specify exactly what you want back.

- **Perplexity Deep Research** — Specialized research mode
- **OpenAI Deep Research** — Academic synthesis
- **Custom Research Prompts** — Domain-specific research
