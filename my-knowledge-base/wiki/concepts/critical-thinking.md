# Critical Thinking

**Critical Thinking** is the objective analysis of information to form a judgment. In the AI context, it refers to prompting techniques that prioritize truth and accuracy over agreeable but incorrect responses.

## AI Applications

- **Fact-First Responses** — Truth over validation
- **Assumption Questioning** — Challenging premises
- **Multiple Perspectives** — Considering alternatives
- **Evidence-Based** — Requiring citations

## Prompting Techniques

- "Question my assumptions"
- "Critique this, don't just agree"
- "Show me counterarguments"
