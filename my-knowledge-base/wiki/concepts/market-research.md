# Market Research

**Category:** Tool
**Definition:** A systematic process of collecting, analyzing, and interpreting data about markets, consumers, and competitors to inform business decisions.

## Overview

Market research encompasses both traditional methodologies and modern AI-powered approaches. Contemporary tools like [[gemini-3]] enable rapid analysis of market trends to replace expensive analyst subscriptions. Effective market research balances quantitative data with qualitative insights to guide product development, pricing strategies, and customer targeting.
