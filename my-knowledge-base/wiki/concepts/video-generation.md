# Video Generation

**Category:** Technique
**Definition:** The process of creating video content through artificial intelligence, programming frameworks, or computational methods that generate moving visual sequences.

## Overview

Video generation encompasses the technologies and methodologies used to produce video content algorithmically rather than through traditional recording. This field has evolved significantly with advances in AI, enabling systems to synthesize realistic video sequences from text prompts, images, or existing footage. Developers and creators now have access to frameworks like Remotion for code-based video effects, while platforms like Flova AI and Seedance 2.0 are democratizing AI-powered filmmaking tools.
