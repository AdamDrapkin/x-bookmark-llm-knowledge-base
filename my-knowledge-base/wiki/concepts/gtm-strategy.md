# GTM Strategy

**GTM (Go-To-Market) Strategy** is the comprehensive plan for bringing a product or service to market. It includes market research, targeting, positioning, and launch execution.

## Key Components

- **Market Research** — Understanding the landscape
- **TAM Analysis** — Total addressable market sizing
- **ICP Development** — Ideal customer profile
- **Messaging** — Positioning and value prop
- **Channel Strategy** — How to reach customers

## AIApplications

- Research prompts for market analysis
- ICP validation prompts
- Messaging generation
- Keyword/targeting recommendations
