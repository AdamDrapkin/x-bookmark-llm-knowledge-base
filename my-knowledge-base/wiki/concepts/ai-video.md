# Ai Video

**Category:** Technique
**Definition:** AI video refers to video content generated or significantly enhanced through artificial intelligence systems, using machine learning models to create, edit, or stylize visual media.

## Overview

AI video encompasses the generation and manipulation of video content using artificial intelligence models. These systems, often built on transformer architectures similar to those used in large language models, can interpret text prompts to produce video sequences, apply cinematic styles, or enhance footage with automated effects. The technology enables creators to generate visual content without traditional video production equipment.
