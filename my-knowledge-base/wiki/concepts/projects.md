# Projects

**Category:** Method
**Definition:** A structured approach to organizing and managing work efforts toward specific goals with defined scope, resources, and timelines.

## Overview

Projects serve as the fundamental organizational unit for completing complex tasks and initiatives. They provide a framework for setting objectives, allocating resources, tracking progress, and achieving deliverables. Effective project management ensures work remains focused, coordinated, and aligned with overarching priorities.
