# Patrick Winston

**Category:** Technique
**Definition:** Patrick Winston was an MIT professor renowned for his influential lectures on communication and public speaking, particularly his viral talk on what separates skilled speakers from the rest.

## Overview

Patrick Winston was a professor of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at MIT, where he taught for over four decades. His communication lecture, which has amassed over 18 million views, challenges conventional wisdom about what makes an effective speaker. The core insight suggests that being the smartest person in the room may actually hinder effective communication rather than help it.
