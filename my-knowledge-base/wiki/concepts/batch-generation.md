# Batch Generation

**Category:** Tool
**Definition:** An AI-powered production method that rapidly creates large volumes of advertising visual assets through automated node-based processing and prompt orchestration.

## Overview

Batch Generation refers to the capability of creating multiple advertising images simultaneously through AI-driven workflows. OpenCreator implements this through a node-based canvas architecture that processes product information and generates hundreds of marketing visuals in approximately 40 seconds. This technique enables mass ad production without manual individual asset creation.
