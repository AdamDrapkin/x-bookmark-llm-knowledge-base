# Building A Second Brain

**Category:** Method
**Definition:** A personal knowledge management system that externalizes information, ideas, and insights from your mind into a trusted digital repository for later retrieval and creative reuse.

## Overview

Building a Second Brain is a methodology developed by productivity expert Tiago Forte for capturing, organizing, distilling, and expressing information to amplify creative output. The system transforms how you interact with digital information by creating an external structure that supplements your biological memory and cognitive capacity.
