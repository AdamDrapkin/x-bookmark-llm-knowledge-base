# Bootstrapped Startup

**Category:** Method
**Definition:** A bootstrapped startup is a company founded and funded primarily through personal finances, early revenues, and resourcefulness rather than external venture capital or significant investor funding.

## Overview

Bootstrapping has become an increasingly viable path to building successful companies, particularly in the current era where access to powerful development tools, AI assistants, and cloud infrastructure has dramatically reduced the cost of building and launching products. Founders maintain full ownership and control while validating their business model through real customer revenue from day one.
