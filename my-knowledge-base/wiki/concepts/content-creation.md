# Content Creation

**Category:** Method
**Definition:** The process of generating and publishing digital content such as videos, text, or images to build an audience and generate revenue.

## Overview

Content creation involves producing and distributing valuable material across digital platforms to attract, engage, and monetize an audience. Successful creators often employ aggressive posting strategies and rapid experimentation to gain traction and build momentum. The practice has become a viable pathway to generating substantial income through various monetization methods.
