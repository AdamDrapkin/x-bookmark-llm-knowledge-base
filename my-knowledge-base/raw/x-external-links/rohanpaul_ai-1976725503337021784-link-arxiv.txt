## Content

**Paper Title**: "Mind Your Tone: Investigating How Prompt Politeness Affects LLM Accuracy"

**Key Findings**:
- Very polite and polite tones REDUCED accuracy
- Neutral, rude, and very rude tones IMPROVED accuracy
- Statistical tests confirmed differences were significant, not random
- Top score: 84.8% for very rude prompts
- Lowest score: 80.8% for very polite prompts
- Older models (GPT-3.5, Llama-2) behaved differently
- GPT-4-based models (ChatGPT-4o) show clear reversal where harsh tone works better
