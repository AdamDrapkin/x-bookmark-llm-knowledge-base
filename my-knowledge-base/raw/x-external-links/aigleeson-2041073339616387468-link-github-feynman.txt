## Content

This is the GitHub repository for **Feynman**, an open-source AI research agent.

### Key Features:
- Multi-agent research system (Researcher, Reviewer, Writer, Verifier)
- Searches papers, synthesizes findings, verifies every claim against real sources
- Generates cited research briefs
- Can replicate experiments on local/cloud GPUs
- MIT License

### From the README:
Feynman is not a chatbot or summary tool—it's a full multi-agent research system that runs from your terminal. Type a topic, and it searches papers, synthesizes findings, verifies claims, and hands you a cited research brief.
